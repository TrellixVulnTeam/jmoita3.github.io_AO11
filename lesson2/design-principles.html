DESIGN PRINCIPLES

By Joao Moita

WHITE SPACE

Tesla

Tesla.com

The principle of white space is exemplified in this image by how clean and simplistic it is overall. There is a lot of clear space arount the vehicle and the text, but it works strongly in bringing your attention to what is most important.

CONTRAST

Studio Simpati

StudioSimpati.co

This website has a great use of contrast. The really dark background switches to a white background when a user scrolls down. We can also see light text pop up in these dark backgrounds, while dark text pops up in the light ones.

REPETITION

Weight Watchers

WeightWatchers.com

The weight watchers website is an excellent example of repetition. In just this screenshot, we can see how the blue buttons are repeated throughout, as well as how each person's success is repeated in each of the four squares.
