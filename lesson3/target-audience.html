Site Audience

While the website will give general weather information for anyone to see, it will be very helpful to those who want to know what the weather conditions are for traveling long distances. These are people who would want to know snow, rain, and other conditions that may be on the road.

Persona 1

Jeff has been planning to drive from Arizona to Montana to take a vacation for him and his family. He has little experience driving in the snow, so Jeff checks weather sidekick so he may be aware of the safest time to travel and avoid the harshest weather.

Persona 2

Jackson is originally from a very dry area. He recently moved to Washington where it rains frequently, so he tunes in to weather sidekick to see what days it would be most ideal to participate in outdoor activities.
